Meridian — Align your next move

Local-first AI career operations platform

Meridian is an open-source, local-first AI career agent I built and host at vijayptiwari.github.io/meridian. Search jobs, score fit, tailor resumes, and assist with applications on LinkedIn and Naukri — with your data staying on your machine. No account. No cloud upload.

  • Multi-agent pipeline: research → fit scoring → resume tailoring → prep
  • Local dashboard with run persistence, checkpoint resume, and demo mode
  • BYOK LLM — OpenAI-compatible APIs, Ollama, Groq, Azure, or keyword fallback
  • Three workspaces: Find jobs · Upskilling · Career transition
