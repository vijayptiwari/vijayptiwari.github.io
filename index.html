Local-first AI career operations platform

Meridian searches jobs, scores fit, tailors resumes, and assists with applications on LinkedIn and Naukri. Your data never leaves your machine. No account. No cloud upload.

  • Multi-agent pipeline: research → fit scoring → resume tailoring → prep
  • Local dashboard with checkpoint resume, run persistence, and demo mode
  • BYOK LLM — OpenAI-compatible, Ollama, Groq, Azure, or keyword fallback
  • Three workspaces: Find jobs · Upskilling · Career transition
